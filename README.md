# FUTURE_DS_01
Customer Retention and Sales Analysis Dashboard using Excel
Sales & Customer Retention Analysis (Task 1)
Objective
This project analyses sales performance and customer retention patterns to identify churn behaviour and provide business recommendations.

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Data Cleaning & Transformation

Key Insights
The business generated over 2.29 million in total revenue
Customers with more orders are significantly more likely to remain active
One-time buyers have the highest churn rate
Revenue is concentrated among repeat customers

Churn & Retention Analysis
Customers who make multiple purchases show stronger retention
A significant portion of customers churn after limited engagement
High-value customers contribute most to long-term revenue

Recommendations
Encourage repeat purchases through loyalty programs
Target first-time buyers with follow-up campaigns
Re-engage inactive customers early
Focus retention efforts on high-value customers
